Why This Matters

Many small business websites contain simple configuration issues that can increase exposure to automated attacks, account takeover attempts, and email impersonation.

A short security review can identify these risks early and provide clear, practical steps to improve your security posture.

What Tech Cache Reviews

  • Email spoofing protection issues such as weak or missing DMARC, SPF, and DKIM enforcement
  • Publicly accessible WordPress admin portals and login exposure
  • Username enumeration through common WordPress endpoints
  • Missing browser security protections such as HSTS and other HTTP security headers
  • Basic website security posture observations for small business websites
